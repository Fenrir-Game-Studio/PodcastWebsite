The Best Places to Travel in 2021

Are you looking for some inspiration for your next vacation? Look no further! Here are the top 5 places to travel in 2021:

  1. Japan - with the Olympics being held in Tokyo this year, Japan is a must-visit destination for sports fans and culture enthusiasts alike.
  2. Costa Rica - this Central American country is known for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and incredible wildlife.
  3. Italy - from the historic city of Rome to the picturesque Amalfi Coast, Italy has something for everyone.
  4. New Zealand - with its breathtaking landscapes and outdoor activities, New Zealand is a dream destination for adventure seekers.
  5. South Africa - experience the beauty of the African savannah and see the "Big Five" on a safari.

So what are you waiting for? Start planning your next adventure today!
